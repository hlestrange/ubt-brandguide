Brand Guide

Welcome to the Unboxed Brand Guide, where you can learn all about how to use things like our logo, colors, and typography. These guidelines should be followed closely for all things Unboxed.

Our Logo

The Unboxed Logo was redesigned to be flat, with just two solid colors. Do not use the old logo. Always use the version of the logo that includes the word "technology".

Use a the flat one-color white version of the logo when the colors clash with surrounding colors, or when the logo needs to be subtle.

Use a the flat one-color logo in black or dark grey (#424242) on a light background.

In some cases, it's okay to use the version of the logo with just the box. Use this version only when the word "Unboxed" is already present or is implied.
